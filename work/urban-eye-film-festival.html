UrbanEye
Film Festival

UrbanEye is a platform for ideas based on the link between the city and cinema, opening the dialogue about the places we live in.
A film festival, held annually in Bucharest since 2013 and in Cluj since 2022, offers screenings accompanied by guided tours, curated film selections, based on current international topics, talks and workshops about the living environment.

Each year the festival has a different theme. The key visual is a reflection upon this theme.

2022 - Transform

If cities and the environment we live in have always been changing, the speed at which it is happening now is unprecedented. As a reflection on what is happening, but also a call to action, the theme of this year’s edition was “Transform”. We question to whom cities belong to and who can still live in them, and propose films that investigate the transformations brought about by gentrification, tourism or migration.

2023 - Where to? Bucharest

UrbanEye Film Festival celebrates its 10-year anniversary edition with the provocative theme “Where to?”, between November 8 to 12 at Cinema Elvire Popesco.
This anniversary moment is an occasion for introspection and reflection on our future. Looking back at our achievements and challenges, we now look to the future with curiosity and hope. This edition invites you to explore the changes that have shaped our cities, how society is redefining itself, and the great global challenges of the moment.
