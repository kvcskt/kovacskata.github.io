Quanta

You start with a sip and the tingling begins to unfold into interfering waves of taste. Quanta, the plural form of quantum, represents the interaction of indivisible units emerging to form a whole, a sum greater than its parts. The brand was an experiment created as part of my studies for a fictional wine family.

role: visual identity, packaging design
