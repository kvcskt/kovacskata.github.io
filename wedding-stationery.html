wedding stationery

A good friend of mine was getting married and he asked me to design the wedding stationery. They wanted something elegant and simple, that's why they chose the eucalypt as the main motive.
First I painted some eucalypts with watercolour and created the key visual. Then based on this, I designed the invitation, table numbers, sitting plan, the menu and event timeline, to which custom illustrations were added.
